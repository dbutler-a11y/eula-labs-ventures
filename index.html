5-Tier Contribution Scoring

Compute is king. Infrastructure investors earn the highest multiplier because they provide the foundation everything else runs on. Tokens are non-transferable — earned through contribution only.

Tier Contribution Type Multiplier Examples Governance Weight
Tier 1 Infrastructure Investment 5x Servers, GPUs, cloud credits, capital Highest voting power
Tier 2 Compute Donation 3x Spare compute, bandwidth, storage High voting power
Tier 3 Agents & Code 2x AI agents, tools, integrations Standard voting power
Tier 4 Research & Knowledge 1.5x Papers, documentation, tutorials Advisory voting
Tier 5 Community & Governance 1x Moderation, events, onboarding Participation voting

Financial Steward = Tier 1 Contributor. Managing fund oversight and compliance for the portfolio qualifies as infrastructure investment — the highest tier. Infrastructure investments also decay at half speed (floor 0.5 vs 0.3), meaning your contribution score compounds over time.
